Just type what happened.

In Taglish. In your own words. However you naturally talk about the store. Litro's AI reads it, records it, and confirms what it understood.

Works on your phone. Works on your staff's phone. The owner gets notified when a report comes in — no extra setup needed.

1

Owner sets up the store on Messenger

Takes 2 minutes. Name the store, get a setup code, share it with your staff. No new app, no training, no device needed.

2

Staff send daily reports by just typing

At the end of the shift, staff message Litro with what happened — sales, expenses, what's running low. In Taglish. Naturally.

3

Litro reads it, records it, notifies the owner

The AI parses the message, confirms what it understood, saves the data, and sends a summary to the owner instantly.

4

See insights on your dashboard

Daily totals, expense tracking, low stock alerts, and trends — all from the data your staff already sends. Coming soon as a web dashboard.
